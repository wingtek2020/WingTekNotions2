About Us

Who are we?

WingTek Notions has partnered with Ravelry. It's am opportunity to meet friends, find inpirations, and most importantly get more crafting done.

By signing up, you will get daily, weekly, or monthly inspirations (free patterns) based on your needle and yarn stash. In Additional You can also get your pattern inspirations from articles such as free scarves as shown below.

You manage your inpirations. You can opt out anytime. You determine your own destiny.

We are also working on a messaging piece that will allow you to see your friends online, chat with them, and share your patterns. Of course we encourage uploading your progress!!!
